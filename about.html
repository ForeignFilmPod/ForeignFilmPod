About Foreign Film Frequency

Tired of Hollywood? Good. Us too.

Foreign Film Frequency is an international film podcast exploring cinema beyond the usual canon. We dive into cult classics, hidden gems, and films that offer something different — visually, culturally, and emotionally.

Each episode takes you into a new cinematic world, with reflections, discussions, and a deep appreciation for storytelling across borders.

Sit back, relax, and queue the subtitles.
